Welcome to pulsED!

pulsED is a web-based app for teachers and school leaders to access quick, research-backed book summaries and practical teaching tips.

How to Use:

  1. Browse books from the homepage.
  2. Click a book to view its summary and key teaching tips.
  3. Take a quick quiz for each book to check your understanding.
  4. Return home to browse and learn more!

Swap in your own book covers and logo for a fully branded experience.
